Website Specification Assignment

  • What is the purpose of this website?
  • Who is the intended audience?
  • What tone do you want for the site’s written content?
  • What will be the primary content of your site?
  • What will be the secondary content?
  • How will users navigate through your site?

What is the purpose of this website?

The main goal of the website is to show my work to potential employers. I plan on making a portfolio website to showcase the work and projects I have done in the past.

Who is the intended audience?

I want to use this portfolio to focus on showing my work to potential employers. I could also use this website to show my work to admissions staff for graduate school.

What tone do you want for the site’s written content?

I would like to create a site that has a professional tone. Since this website is aimed at employers, I would like to come across as formal but not dry.

What will be the primary content of your site?

I would like the site to have a minimal aesthetic. The landing page should be clean with a simple title, navigation, and maybe a few images. I would want to share some writing work, presentations, and creative work.

What will be the secondary content?

I plan on including pages for the following sections: about me, contact, resume, school work, and personal work. Each section is fairly self-explanitory and simple.

How will users navigate through your site?

The landing page will have a menu where the user can select one of the previously stated pages. When opened, each page will have work and navigation that leads to other pages.
